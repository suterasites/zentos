Ask a question
What kind of projects does Zentos take on?

We focus on three things we do well: multi-unit developments, homes on sloping or constrained blocks, and one-off architectural builds. If your project sits across a couple of those, it is usually a good fit for us.

Do you work alongside my architect and designer?

Yes. A lot of our work comes from homes that have already been designed, and our job is to build them the way they were drawn, protecting the detail rather than trimming it back. We are just as happy to come in early and work through documentation with your team.

Can you build on a difficult or sloping site?

Sloping and tight sites are one of our specialities. They take more planning and a builder who is comfortable with the engineering, which is exactly the kind of project we like to take on.

How involved will I be during the build?

As involved as you want to be. Because we deliberately keep our numbers small, you deal directly with the people actually running your build, so questions get answered quickly and you always know where things are up to.

Which areas do you build in?

We are based in Docklands and build across metropolitan Melbourne. If you are not sure whether your site is in our area, get in touch and we will let you know.

How do we get started?

The easiest first step is a conversation about your site, your plans and what you are hoping to build. Send through the enquiry form or give us a call, and we will take it from there.
